I am a PhD Candidate at the Hubbard School of Journalism and Mass Communication at the University of Minnesota. In my research I focus on women's sports media coverage, digital platforms, and audience practices. I've published my award winning work in [Media, Culture & Society](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/01634437261417330), [Communication and Sport](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/21674795241301308), and the [Sociology of Sport Journal](https://journals.humankinetics.com/view/journals/ssj/41/4/article-p397.xml). I have experience teaching classes on the role of digital media in society, popular culture, and sports media and information.

My current dissertation project explores how U.S.-based women's sports audiences access and encounter women's sports media and the rising presence of women's sports bars across the U.S.
